# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes customer shopping behavior for a retail company to uncover insights that improve sales, customer engagement, and business strategy.

The project includes:
- Data Cleaning & EDA (Python)
- Data Analysis (SQL)
- Dashboard Visualization (Power BI)

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
How can the company leverage customer shopping data to:
- Identify purchasing trends
- Improve customer engagement
- Optimize marketing and product strategies

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Python** (Pandas, EDA)
- **SQL** (MySQL)
- **Power BI** (Dashboard Visualization)

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Customer shopping behavior dataset (`customer_shopping_behavior.csv`)

---

## 🔍 Key SQL Analysis
- Revenue by gender
- Impact of discounts on spending
- Top-rated products
- Shipping type comparison
- Subscriber vs non-subscriber behavior
- Discount usage patterns
- Customer segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)
- Top products per category
- Repeat buyers vs subscription
- Revenue by age group

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights
- Total Revenue: **$233K**
- Total Customers: **3.9K**
- Avg Rating: **3.75**
- Subscription Split: **73% Non-Subscribers, 27% Subscribers**
- Customer Segments:
  - Returning: 52%
  - Loyal: 31%
  - New: 16%
- Top Products: Sweater, Sunglasses, Socks

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Discounts significantly influence purchase behavior
- Returning customers form the largest segment
- Subscribers contribute higher overall revenue
- Certain categories consistently perform better across seasons
